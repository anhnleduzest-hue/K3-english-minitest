VOCABULARY & GRAMMAR MASTERY

Ms.Linh Anh's English Lesson

I. VOCABULARY: UNIT 1 WORDS

1. We play football and run in the (p _ _ y _ _ _ _ _ d).
2. Monkeys are very good at (c _ _ m _ _ _ g) trees.
3. Can you (h _ _) like a frog?
4. Look! The girls are (s _ _ p _ _ _ g) with a rope.
5. I like to (r _ _ l _ r s _ _ t _) in the park.
6. Please (m _ _ e) to the front of the class.
7. We (t _ _ k) with our friends every day.
8. The children (w _ _ k) to school together.
9. Let's (w _ _ c h) TV after dinner.
10. The boy is (j _ _ p _ _ g) up and down.

II. PRESENT CONTINUOUS (am/is/are + V-ing)

11. (+) Look! The chef (stir) the soup.
12. (+) Listen! The birds (sing) loudly.
13. (+) I (walk) to the park right now.
14. (+) The octopus (sit) on the high chair.
15. (+) We (have) a great time.
16. (-) The dog (not/swim) in the water.
17. (-) I (not/watch) TV.
18. (-) They (not/run) round the park.
19. (-) The man (not/sleep) on the bed.
20. (-) She (not/skip) in the yard.
21. (?) the walrus (play) the guitar?
22. (?) you (do) your homework?
23. (?) the mice (dance) near the pool?
24. (?) Nam (climb) the ladder?
25. (?) they (move) to the new class?

III. BE GOOD AT & LIKE + V-ING

26. (+) I am good at (draw) funny pictures.
27. (+) My sister likes (read) comic books.
28. (+) They are good at (speak) English.
29. (+) He likes (take) photos.
30. (+) We are good at (swim) in the pool.
31. (-) I (not/be good at) climbing walls.
32. (-) She (not/like) watching scary movies.
33. (-) The students (not/be good at) roller skating.
34. (-) My brother (not/like) doing the laundry.
35. (-) You (not/be good at) hopping.
36. (Lan / like) (talk) to her teacher.
37. (My friends / not / be good at) (play) football.
38. (The cat / love) (chase) the butterfly.
39. (You / like) (listen) to pop music?
40. (I / not / like) (walk) in the rain.
